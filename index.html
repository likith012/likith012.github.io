IHUB DATA — Research Associate

A research wing of IIIT, Hyderabad with focus on data driven technologies, responsible for applying deep learning in the field of biosignals to create a broader impact in healthcare.

  • Worked on modelling multi-modal biosignals coming from diverse devices efficiently and effectively in collaboration with NIMHANS, Banglore, and several physicians.
  • Responsible for developing a reliable tool for automating a person’s sleep analysis and also to understand its impact on his/her cognitive ability.
  • Involved in designing experiments on subjects for in-house data collection adhering to ethical standards.

  • A low resource model with 12 times decrease in model footprint and 75% improvement in inference time when compared to the current best was designed. A knowledge distillation pipeline that uses only a subset of data is employed to obtain an improvement of 3.4%.

  • The explainable visualizations obtained from our tool were helpful for better human-AI interaction.
